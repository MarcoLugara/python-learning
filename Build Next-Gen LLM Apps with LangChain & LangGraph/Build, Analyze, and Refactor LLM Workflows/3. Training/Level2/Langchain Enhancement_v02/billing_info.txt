Billing and Payment Information

Payment Methods:
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also use purchase orders and bank transfers. All prices are in USD unless otherwise specified.

Billing Cycles:
Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually depending on your plan. Monthly subscriptions renew on the same day each month. Annual subscriptions offer a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.

Invoice Disputes:
If you notice an incorrect charge, contact billing@company.com within 30 days. Include your invoice number and a description of the issue. Most disputes are resolved within 5 business days.

Late Payments:
A $25 late fee is applied to accounts with payments overdue by more than 10 days. Enterprise accounts have Net-30 payment terms with no late fees if paid within the term.

Refund Processing:
Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Credit card refunds appear within 2-3 billing cycles. PayPal refunds are typically instant. Bank transfer refunds may take 7-10 business days.

Tax Information:
Sales tax is applied based on your billing address. International customers may be subject to VAT or GST. Enterprise customers can submit a tax exemption certificate to waive applicable taxes.

Premium Tier Pricing:
The premium tier is $49/month for individual accounts or $299/month for enterprise accounts with up to 50 users. Additional users are $5 each per month.
