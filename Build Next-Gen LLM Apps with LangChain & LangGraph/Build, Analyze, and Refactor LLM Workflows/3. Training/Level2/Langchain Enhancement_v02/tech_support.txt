Technical Support Guidelines

Support Hours:
Our technical support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 priority support through our dedicated hotline.

Common Issues:

Router Connectivity:
If your router loses connection frequently, first try a factory reset by holding the reset button for 10 seconds. Then reconfigure using the setup wizard in the admin panel at 192.168.1.1. If issues persist, check for firmware updates.

Software Installation Errors:
Installation errors are often caused by insufficient permissions. Run the installer as administrator (Windows) or with sudo (Linux/Mac). Ensure your system meets the minimum requirements: 8GB RAM, 2GB free disk space.

Account Access Issues:
For password resets, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you don't receive the reset email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. Enterprise users should contact their IT administrator for SSO-related issues.

License Activation:
Each license key can be activated on up to 3 devices. Deactivate unused devices through your account dashboard before activating on a new device. License keys are case-sensitive.

Performance Optimization:
If the application runs slowly, clear the cache from Settings > Advanced > Clear Cache. Close unnecessary background applications. For databases over 1GB, consider enabling the performance mode in preferences.
