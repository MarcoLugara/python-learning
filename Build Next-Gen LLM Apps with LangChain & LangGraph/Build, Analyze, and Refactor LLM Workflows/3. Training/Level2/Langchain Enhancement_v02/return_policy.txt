Company Return Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Standard Return Policy:
All products can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Products must be in original packaging and unused condition. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the product is defective.

Defective Products:
For defective products, we offer free return shipping and a full refund or replacement. Customers must report defects within 14 days of delivery. We will provide a prepaid shipping label for the return.

Enterprise Customers:
Enterprise customers with active contracts have extended return windows of 60 days. All return shipping is covered by the company for enterprise accounts. Custom orders may have different terms specified in the contract.

Digital Products:
Digital products and software licenses are non-refundable once the license key has been activated. However, if a technical issue prevents usage, customers can contact support for troubleshooting or a refund consideration within 7 days.

Restocking Fee:
A 15% restocking fee applies to opened electronic items that are not defective. This fee is waived for enterprise customers.
